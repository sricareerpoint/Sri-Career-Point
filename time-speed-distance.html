Question 1

A train travels 240 km in 4 hours. Its speed is:

A) 50 km/h
B) 60 km/h
C) 70 km/h
D) 80 km/h

Show Answer

Explanation: Speed = Distance ÷ Time = 240 ÷ 4 = 60 km/h.
